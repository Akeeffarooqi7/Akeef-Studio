Privacy Policy

Akeef · Studio ("the studio", "we", "I") respects your privacy. This page explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how it is handled. If anything below is unclear, please write to akeef.farooqi@gmail.com and we will respond promptly.

1. Information we collect

We only collect information that you knowingly provide:

  • Your name, email address, project type and budget when you submit the contact form.
  • Any additional details you choose to share in your message or in subsequent emails.
  • Basic, anonymised analytics (e.g. page views) to understand how visitors use the site.

2. How we use it

We use this information solely to respond to inquiries, prepare proposals, and deliver projects you have engaged us for. We do not sell, rent or trade your personal data.

3. How it's stored

Inquiry emails are stored in our standard email provider. Project files and assets are stored in encrypted cloud workspaces, accessible only to people directly involved in your project. We retain communications only as long as needed to support active and past clients.

4. Third-party services

We may use third-party tools to help operate the studio — for example, hosting providers, analytics tools, and email services. These vendors are bound by their own privacy policies and are selected for their security posture.

5. Your rights

You can request a copy of any personal data we hold about you, ask for it to be corrected, or ask for it to be deleted. Email us and we'll action it within a reasonable timeframe.

6. Cookies

This website uses minimal functional cookies (if any) and may use a privacy-respecting analytics cookie. No cookies are used for advertising or cross-site tracking.

7. Contact

For any privacy-related question or request, write to akeef.farooqi@gmail.com.
